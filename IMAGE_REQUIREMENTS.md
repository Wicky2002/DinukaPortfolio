## Image Recommendations

### For Best Results:
1. **Profile Photos**: High-quality, professional headshots with good lighting
2. **Project Images**: 
   - Use screenshots for software projects
   - Use actual photos for hardware projects
   - Ensure images are clear and high-resolution
   - Minimum resolution: 800x600px
   - Preferred formats: JPG or PNG
